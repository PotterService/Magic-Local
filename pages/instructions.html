Instructions

How to use your local collector.

Your saves are stored on your device, not online.

Important

Export backups often so you do not lose your collection.

Local Saves

Nothing you save is stored online by this site. Owned counts, foil counts, notes, conditions, and want-list marks are saved locally in your browser using IndexedDB.

If you clear browser data, switch browsers, use another computer, or your browser storage is reset, your collection can be lost unless you export a backup.

Basic Use

  1. Go to Home.
  2. Click Pull Magic Sets.
  3. Choose a set and click Load Cards.
  4. Use + and − buttons to mark owned and foil counts.
  5. Use Want Card to add cards to your want list.
  6. Use Owned to view only cards you own.

Backups

Use the Backup or Settings page to export your collection. Keep the exported file somewhere safe.

Storage Cleanup

The Settings menu lets you remove sets/cards to save space. You can remove cards that are not owned while keeping cards marked as owned. Do not worry: you can still redownload those sets later with the Redownload Set button.

Redownloading Sets

If you cleaned storage and only owned cards remain, open the set and choose Redownload Full Set, or use the Redownload Set button from Home when shown.
