Terms and Usage

Terms for Bow MTG Static Collector.

A simple fan-made local collection tracker.

Privacy

Your collection data is stored locally in your browser, not on this website.

Fan Project Notice

This is a fan-made collector tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed, sponsored, or specifically approved by Wizards of the Coast, Hasbro, MTGJSON, or Scryfall. Magic: The Gathering names, card data, and card images belong to their respective owners.

Local Data

Your saved owned counts, foil counts, notes, conditions, and want-list marks are stored locally in your browser. They are not submitted online by this static website.

Backups Are Your Responsibility

You should export your collection regularly. If browser storage is cleared or lost, your saved collection may be lost.

Data Accuracy

Card information, images, and prices may be incomplete, outdated, or unavailable. Always verify important values before buying, selling, or trading.

Use At Your Own Risk

This tool is provided as-is for personal collection tracking.
